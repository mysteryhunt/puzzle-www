MIT Brass Rat Hunt

The 2027 MIT Brass Rat hunt was a puzzlehunt written for Brass Rat Premiere, the annual sophomore class ring event. The prize for winning was a free class ring.

The hunt took place February 2025; note that the title of the hunt refers to the graduation year of the sophomores competing, not the year the hunt was held.
